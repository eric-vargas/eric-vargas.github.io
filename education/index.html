Armbrust Real Estate Institute (AREI)

Associate Broker, FA Credential - Pre-Licensing Education (168 hours)

Expected completion: December 2020

Colorado requires completion of six courses and successful passing of the state exam prior to issuance of a license. The state exam is comprised of two sections, the General real estate section and the Colorado section. AREI is consistently ranked highest for state exam passing rates.

At AREI, we consider ourselves in partnership with our students. It’s all about success in attaining your career goals. Our purpose is to provide you a personalized learning environment where you can receive the assistance you need. We offer extensive expertise, counseling and career guidance along with a myriad of additional post license resources.

Real Estate Law & Practice

Armbrust Real Estate Institute (AREI)

This course encompasses 48 hours of the required 168 hours for initial licensing for real estate brokers. The course content introduces the practice of real estate through a study of a common body of knowledge, including key real estate terms and concepts as well as related federal laws practices in a majority of states. This course is necessary to prepare for the general portion of the state exam.

Colorado Contracts & Regulations

Armbrust Real Estate Institute (AREI)

This course encompasses 48 hours of the required 168 hours for initial licensing for real estate brokers. The course content includes an in-depth study of Colorado statutes, Real Estate Commission rules and mandatory contracts that govern the industry. Also included is extensive practice in preparation of Real Estate Commission approved forms. This course is necessary to prepare for the state portion of the state exam.

Trust Accounts & Record Keeping

Armbrust Real Estate Institute (AREI)

This course encompasses 8 of the required 168 hours for initial licensing for real estate brokers. The course content includes instruction in proper record keeping procedures as required by the Colorado Real Estate Commission to maintain and account for funds belonging to others. This course is necessary to prepare for the state portion of the state exam.

Real Estate Closings

Armbrust Real Estate Institute (AREI)

This course encompasses 24 hours of the required 168 hours for initial licensing for real estate brokers. The course content includes instruction in the skills necessary to properly close a real estate transaction. Learned skills include an agent’s responsibilities from the contract through and after the closing, plus accounting for all funds received and disbursed, as well as a comprehensive review of the legal documents prepared by the broker. This course is necessary to prepare for the state portion of the state exam.

Practical Applications

Armbrust Real Estate Institute (AREI)

This course encompasses 32 hours of the required 168 hours for initial licensing for real estate brokers. The course covers the practical aspects of real estate brokerage. This is accomplished by providing a program of study for new real estate brokers in the application of laws, rules and standards of practice including entering the real estate field, establishing brokerage relationships, showing property, preparing and presenting offers, and closing real estate transactions.
